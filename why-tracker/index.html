Ask Yourself... IF NOT NOW? WHEN?
Take a moment to truly dig deep and answer these questions. The more honest you are, the more powerful your "Why" will be.
1. What are you tired of in your current health journey? (Be brutally honest)
2. How will your daily life be different once you achieve your goals? How will you feel?
3. Who are you doing this for? (Is it for your family, your future self, or to prove something to yourself?)
4. If you don’t make this change, what is the biggest regret you will have in one year? In five years?
5. What is the one non-negotiable commitment you will make to yourself right now?
6. Your Personal WHY: Write your “Why” below as a phrase, sentence, or short paragraph.
“My why is louder than my excuses.”
“I’m not chasing change—I’m becoming it.”
“Every action I take waters the future I’ve planted.”
